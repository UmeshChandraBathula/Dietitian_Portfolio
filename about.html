My Story

Why I became a
Nutrition & Food Therapy Consultant

My journey into nutrition wasn't a straight line — it started in a pharmacy lab. After completing my Bachelor's and Master's in Pharmacy at Manipal College of Pharmaceutical Sciences, I kept seeing the same pattern: people managing chronic conditions with medication alone, when the most powerful intervention was sitting on their plate.

That realization changed everything for me. I went back to study what I knew was missing — earning my Diploma in Food Therapy & Nutrition from the National Board of Vocational Training Education (NBVTE), a vocational board under the Ministry of MSME. That dual background became my biggest advantage as a practitioner: advice that is both rigorously science-backed and completely free of diet-culture noise.

I launched NUme to offer the kind of individualized, compassionate care I believe everyone deserves — where food is medicine, and your plate is the first line of defence. Whether you're navigating a health condition, rebuilding your relationship with food, or simply tired of feeling less than your best, you deserve a plan built on real science. That's exactly what I'm here for.
