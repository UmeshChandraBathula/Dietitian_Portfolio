A holistic approach to wellness
using the MPESS framework

To ensure deep and lasting health, I move beyond just the "plate." I adopt a holistic approach using my MPESS framework — addressing the Mental, Physical, Emotional, Social, and Spiritual dimensions of your well-being to truly heal your body from the inside out.

Most nutritionists understand food. I understand food and how your body processes it at a molecular level — because I started my career in pharmaceutical science.

After earning my Masters in Pharmacy at Manipal College of Pharmaceutical Sciences, I kept seeing the same pattern: people managing chronic conditions with medication alone, when the most powerful intervention was sitting on their plate. That realization changed everything for me.

I went back to study what I knew was missing — earning my Diploma in Food Therapy & Nutrition from the National Board of Vocational Training. Today, that dual background is your advantage: advice that is both rigorously science-backed and completely free of diet-culture noise.

Whether you're navigating a health condition, rebuilding your relationship with food, or simply tired of feeling less than your best — you deserve a plan built on real science, not the latest trend. That's exactly what I'm here for.

My Philosophy

You deserve to enjoy food,
not fear it

I don't believe in one-size-fits-all diets. My approach is rooted in balance, self-compassion, and the science of sustainable change.

🔬

Evidence-Based Practice

Every recommendation I make is grounded in the latest nutrition science — not trends, not fads. Real research, translated into practical advice for your daily life.

🤝

Non-Diet, Non-Judgmental

I meet you exactly where you are — no shame, no guilt. We focus on building sustainable habits, not following rigid rules that leave you feeling deprived.

🌿

Whole Person Wellness

Food is just one piece of the puzzle. We also look at sleep, stress, movement, and lifestyle — because your health is so much more than what's on your plate.

🎯

Goal-Centered Planning

Your goals drive everything. Whether it's managing a condition, improving energy, or rebuilding your relationship with food — we build your plan around what matters to you.

📱

Ongoing Support

Change happens between sessions too. I'm available for questions and encouragement because I know that navigating everyday nutrition decisions can be challenging.

🧭

Long-Term Thinking

I'm not here to give you a quick fix. I'm here to help you build lasting habits and a healthier relationship with food that serves you for years to come.
